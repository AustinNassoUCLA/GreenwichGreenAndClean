Dear Neighbor,

As each season puts its imprint on Greenwich, so does Greenwich Green & Clean. Since our founding in 1986, we have remained committed to developing partnerships to beautify the public spaces we all share. Our projects include hanging summer and winter baskets in Central Greenwich, landscaping and maintaining town highway exits, monitoring town-wide clean ups and overseeing recycling programs.

The members of Green and Clean invite you to take a walk with us through Greenwich and meet our devoted partners. We hope that one you see the role we play in this community, you will be inspired to support our worthy cause.

Best Regards,
Jane D. Marsh
Chairman, Greenwich Green and Clean
