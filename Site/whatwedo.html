What do we do?

  • Educate children on recycling and preservation of the environment
  • Landscape traffic islands
  • Organize town-wide cleanup days
  • Plant trees, shrubs and flowers
  • Hang baskets in Central Greenwich and Cos Cob
  • Landscape and maintain State highway
  • Beautify Town traffic islands and parks
  • Award prizes for beautification of public space
  • Teach and implement recycling
  • Distribute daffodil bulbs
  • Remove invasive vines in town parks
  • Dispose of graffiti
  • Coordinate private gifts for public property
  • Participate in Town planning

Where can you find us at work?

Greenwich Green & Clean is committed to the concept of public/private partnerships: the principle of “shared ownership” of public space. Our daily business is to provide opportunities for volunteers to help with projects, which are not covered by Town or State budgets. Since 1986, we have been cleaning and planting throughout Greenwich. The following is just a tiny portion of the list of sites which we have improved:

  • Greenwich Avenue
  • Holly Hill Resource Recovery Facility
  • Exit 5 near the I-95
  • Cos Cob, near the Library and Strickland Pond
  • Round Hill Road at the Merritt Parkway
  • Tod’s Point
  • Pemberwick Park
